📸 Photo tips

Good lighting makes all the difference. Shoot near a window in natural light, and use a plain background to let the food shine.

✍️ Writing tips

Write steps as if explaining to a friend. Be specific about temperatures, timings and visual cues (e.g. "until golden brown").

🏷️ Tags

Good tags help people discover your recipe. Include cuisine type, main ingredient and occasion (e.g. "weeknight", "party").
